## 2024-05-22 - Theme Toggle Accessibility
**Learning:** Dropdowns for binary choices (Light/Dark) add unnecessary friction and often lack accessibility.
**Action:** Prefer single-click toggle buttons with `aria-label` and meaningful icons for binary states.

## 2024-05-23 - Skip to Content
**Learning:** Skip links are essential for keyboard users to bypass repetitive navigation and access main content immediately.
**Action:** Always include a hidden (sr-only, focus:not-sr-only) "Skip to content" link as the first focusable element.
